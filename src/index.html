How do parking violations affect me?

Parking violations and their associated fines cost Chicagoans thousands of dollars every year.

What are my odds for appealing?

Your odds are great! Only 9% of tickets issued in the 25th ward between Jan. 1, 2013 and Dec. 31, 2017 were contested. However, 3 out of 5 of those appeals resulted in the ticket being thrown out!

There is no fee to request an administrative hearing, and no penalty if you lose. Once you request a hearing, you will receive a week during which to appear at a hearing facility. You can go anytime during that week, based on your availability.

Our site contains information to help you understand the appeal process, prepare your case, and contest your ticket confidently. Go to our allowable defenses page to learn how you can defend yourself. Use our preparation page to build your case and prepare your evidence.
